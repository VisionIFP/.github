# Security Policy

Vision IFP operates in a regulated financial services environment. We take security seriously and appreciate responsible disclosure of vulnerabilities.

---

## Scope

This policy covers all software and infrastructure maintained under this GitHub organisation, including:

- Adviser and client-facing web applications
- Internal tooling and portals
- APIs and integration services
- Infrastructure configuration

---

## Reporting a Vulnerability

**Please do not report security vulnerabilities through public channels.**

If you believe you've found a vulnerability, report it directly to our development team:

📧 **[it@visionifp.co.uk](mailto:it@visionifp.co.uk)**

Include as much of the following as you can:

- A description of the vulnerability and its potential impact
- Steps to reproduce or a proof of concept
- Any relevant URLs, request/response samples, or screenshots
- Your name and contact details (optional, but appreciated)

We treat all reports in confidence.

---

## What to Expect

- **Acknowledgement** within 2 business days
- **Initial assessment** within 5 business days
- We'll keep you informed as we investigate and resolve the issue
- We'll let you know when a fix has been deployed

---

## Responsible Disclosure

We ask that you:

- Give us reasonable time to investigate and remediate before any public disclosure
- Avoid accessing, modifying, or deleting data that isn't yours
- Don't perform actions that could disrupt services or impact other users
- Act in good faith

We commit to not pursuing legal action against researchers who follow these guidelines.

---

## Out of Scope

The following are generally out of scope:

- Social engineering or phishing of staff
- Physical security
- Denial of service attacks
- Issues in third-party services outside our control
- Theoretical vulnerabilities without a working proof of concept

---

## Supported Versions

We actively maintain and patch the current production release of each application. Older or deprecated versions are not supported.
