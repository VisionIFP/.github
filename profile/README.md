# Vision IFP

**The UK's exclusive independent financial adviser network.**

Vision IFP is a directly FCA-authorised network of Appointed Representatives, providing truly independent financial advice to private clients, corporates, charities, trusts, and court of protection clients across the UK. With over <!-- ADVISER_COUNT -->142<!-- /ADVISER_COUNT --> appointed representatives and <!-- FUA -->£6.9 billion<!-- /FUA --> funds under advice, we're one of the country's most established and recognised adviser networks.

---

## What We Do

We provide the ideal home for highly experienced independent financial advisers — giving them the freedom to access the whole of market and the support infrastructure to focus on what matters most: delivering exceptional, unbiased advice to their clients.

Our services span:

- **Investments** — whole of market, independent recommendations
- **Pensions** — planning, consolidation, and retirement strategies
- **Protection** — life, income, and critical illness cover

---

## Our Platform

Alongside our adviser services, Vision operates a suite of bespoke technology built in-house — including a fully integrated back-office system, the [Vision Investment Portal](https://viportaluk.co.uk), and tools that support the entire financial advice process for advisers, clients, and internal teams alike.
